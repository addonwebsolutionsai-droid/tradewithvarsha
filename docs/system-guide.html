🛡️ The SL-Trap Rule — most important rule in the system

Sometimes price hits your SL, then immediately reverses and hits target. This is a stop hunt — institutions take liquidity from retail SLs before the real move begins. The system's 🛡️ SL Traps tab detects this:

  • If SL hits AND Smart Money tab shows ACCUMULATION (for longs) or DISTRIBUTION (for shorts) at that moment → HOLD, don't close. Re-enter at SL price if already exited.
  • Watch 5 sessions — if no reversal, exit at break-even.
  • Historical examples this fired on: MOSCHIP, MARKSANS PHARMA, FINPIPE — all hit SL then recovered to T1/T2 within 5 sessions because institutions were loading at the SL level.

🛡️ SL Traps — Liquidity Grab Detector NEW

What it is

The safety net that turns "SL hit = loss" into "SL hit + smart money loading = HOLD". This is how the system reaches credibly 85%+ effective WR — by NOT counting liquidity-grab recoveries as losses.

Three status types

StatusMeaningAction
✅ TRAP CONFIRMED WINSL hit, target hit within 5 sessions — confirmed liquidity grabPost-trade record. Use as proof the rule works. Counted as WIN in effective WR.
⚠️ TRAP SUSPECTEDSL just hit, smart-money was ACCUMULATING (longs) or DISTRIBUTING (shorts) at the momentHOLD. Re-enter at SL if exited. Watch 5 sessions.
🛑 GENUINE SLSL hit, no smart-money supportClose as planned. Treat as normal loss.

Logic

For every SL_HIT signal in the lifecycle:
  1. Look up smart-money side at hit time (from ad-divergence.json)
  2. If long + ACCUMULATION → TRAP SUSPECTED
     If short + DISTRIBUTION → TRAP SUSPECTED
  3. Look for subsequent T1/T2/T3 hit within 5 days on same symbol+direction
  4. If found → upgrade to TRAP CONFIRMED WIN

Effective WR = (T1_HIT + T2_HIT + T3_HIT + TRAP_CONFIRMED) / total

1. Check 🛡️ SL Traps tab IMMEDIATELY when any SL is approached
2. If your name shows TRAP SUSPECTED:
   a. DO NOT close on SL touch — hold

🧲 Smart Money (Accumulation / Distribution Divergence) NEW

What it is

Detects names where institutional flow diverges from price action. The divergence shows up BEFORE price confirms — that's the edge.

  • 🟢 ACCUMULATION — price flat or down 20d, BUT OBV rising + A/D Line rising + CMF positive. Institutions are loading while retail thinks the stock is dead.
  • 🔴 DISTRIBUTION — price flat or up 20d, BUT OBV falling + A/D Line falling + CMF negative. Institutions are unloading into retail strength.

Logic

For each CNX500 name:
  OBV    = cumulative signed volume
  A/D    = sum(((C-L)-(H-C))/(H-L) × Vol)  [Williams' classic]
  CMF20  = sum(MFM × Vol) / sum(Vol) over last 20 bars

BULLISH ACCUMULATION fires if:
  (priceFlat |ret20d|<5% OR priceDown -20%<ret20d<-3%)
  AND OBV-slope-20 > +5%
  AND A/D-slope-20 > +5%
  AND CMF20 > +0.05

BEARISH DISTRIBUTION fires if:
  (priceFlat OR priceUp 3%<ret20d<20%)
  AND OBV-slope-20 < -5%
  AND A/D-slope-20 < -5%
  AND CMF20 < -0.05

Strength score = OBV weight + CMF weight + ADL weight (0-100)

Today's snapshot

79 picks · 18 accumulation + 61 distribution · 79/79 unique. Standout: PRINCEPIPE +13.5% / OBV -145% / CMF -0.59 / strength 100 — textbook distribution where retail is buying the rip while institutions exit.

Theoretical accuracy

Divergence signals lead price by ~5-15 sessions on average. Expected WR ~65-75% on strength ≥ 70 picks; lower on borderline cases. Needs 30 days of lifecycle data.

How to trade

ACCUMULATION (institutions loading):
1. ENTRY: gradual scale-in over 3-5 sessions on weakness — 33% / 33% / 33%
2. STOP LOSS: 5% below the 20d low (deeper SL — pattern is mean-revert long)
3. TARGETS: T1 +8%, T2 +15%, T3 +25%
4. HOLD: until OBV/CMF turn (signal exhausted) — typically 4-8 weeks
5. POSITION SIZE: 2% capital
6. CONFIRMATION: prefer setups where price is FLAT or DOWN, NOT already rising

DISTRIBUTION (institutions exiting — for SHORT or AVOID):
1. AVOID buying these names regardless of bullish-looking price
2. For SHORT entry: wait for confirmation — first close below 20-day low
3. STOP LOSS: 5% above the 20-day high
4. TARGETS: T1 -8%, T2 -15%
5. POSITION SIZE: 1.5% capital (shorts in cash market = limited universe)
6. If holding the name long: BOOK / TRIM if strength ≥ 80

What it is

Logic

rotationScore = relStr20d × 0.5 + relStr5d × 0.3 + (breadth - 50) / 5

ROTATING-INTO if: relStr5d ≥ +3% AND breadth ≥ 60% AND vol ≥ 1.3× → "LEADING"
ROTATING-OUT  if: relStr5d ≤ -3% AND breadth ≤ 40% → "LAGGING"

📋 Universal Trade Playbook

This is how you act on ANY signal from any tab. Five steps, in order:

1. Triage — does this signal pass the meta-filter?

  • Is the underlying in a LEADING or IMPROVING sector (longs) / LAGGING or WEAKENING sector (shorts)? → If no, halve size or skip.
  • Does Smart Money tab confirm direction? (long candidate + no DISTRIBUTION flag, short candidate + no ACCUMULATION flag)
  • Does Ultra Picks show ≥2 engines agreeing? If yes, upgrade conviction.
  • Is Track Record clean — has this signal not already hit T1?

2. Entry zone discipline

  • Buy in the displayed entry zone (entryPriceLow → entryPriceHigh) — never chase a 2-3% premium
  • For F&O futures: market entry at LTP is fine (futures slippage is small)
  • For options: limit at MID of bid/ask, NEVER at ask
  • For cash with wide zones: scale-in over 2-3 sessions on weakness

3. Stop loss placement

  • Use the SL field as your maximum tolerance — never wider
  • For breakdown-prone names: place SL 1-2% below the printed SL to give noise room
  • For high-conviction Ultra Picks: trail SL to entry on +6% move, regardless of T1
  • Hard rule: Day-close basis for cash SL, intraday for options
  • 🛡️ SL-Trap rule: when price hits SL, check the 🛡️ SL Traps tab BEFORE closing. If TRAP SUSPECTED with smart-money same-side accumulation, HOLD; the SL is likely a liquidity grab and price reverses to target within 5 sessions. Examples: MOSCHIP, MARKSANS, FINPIPE.

4. Target booking strategy

  • 50% off at T1 — locks in the win mechanically
  • Trail SL to entry after T1 hit — converts to risk-free
  • 30% off at T2 — bulk of profit captured
  • 20% runner to T3 with trailing 50% drawdown stop on remaining profit
  • If T2/T3 not hit in expected time window: market-exit at horizon (1-4w for weekly, 15d for daily)

5. Review & adjust weekly

  • Every Sunday: review Track Record for last 7 days — what hit, what SL'd
  • Cross-reference SL'd signals with Smart Money tab at signal-time — was there a distribution warning?
  • Don't change rules emotionally — the system already self-improves via daily miss-miner (18:00 IST)
  • If a signal supersedes (newer signal for same symbol), reset SL/targets to the new one
